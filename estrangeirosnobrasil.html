Therapy for English-speaking foreigners living in Brazil

Hello, I am a Cognitive Behavioral Therapist based in Brazil and I specialize in helping English-speaking foreigners navigate the challenges of living in a new country. As someone who has personally experienced the difficulties of adapting to a new culture, I understand how overwhelming it can be to find your footing in unfamiliar surroundings.

Moving to a new country can be an exciting adventure, but it can also be a very stressful experience. It's not uncommon to feel isolated, anxious, and uncertain about how to navigate a new environment. In these situations, seeking professional support can make all the difference. I believe that therapy can be a powerful tool to help you adjust to your new life and thrive in your new surroundings.

As a Cognitive Behavioral Therapist, my approach focuses on helping clients identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that are holding them back. Through our sessions, I will work with you to develop practical strategies to manage your stress, anxiety, and depression, as well as help you build positive relationships with those around you.

My therapy services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client, and I offer a safe and non-judgmental space where you can share your concerns and work towards your goals. My goal is to help you feel more confident, empowered, and fulfilled in your new life in Brazil.

If you're an English-speaking foreigner in Brazil and are struggling to adjust to your new surroundings, I invite you to contact me to schedule an appointment. Together, we can work towards a brighter, more positive future.
